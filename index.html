Polytechnic Final Year Project (FYP)

Data Analysis & Predictive Modelling of Sport Singapore (SportSG) Academies and Clubs Programmes for Children and Youth

Project Description

My team and I analyzed data regarding Sport Singapore's Youth participants in order to provide insights

on how SportSG can strategize, design and develop sport programs to retain and increase participation rate.
As well as making use of Python's Artificial Intelligence libraries (sklearn,XGBoost) to predict
participant behavior. After understanding and cleaning the data, we created metrics such as the Retention,
Progression, Conversion and Transfer metrics on Python to fully understand their demographics and behavior,
before putting them all in easily understandable storyboards on Tableau to present actionable insights to
Sport Singapore. We created models that predict sport academy member retention and a programme creator model
that can predict the estimated participation based on user inputted factors (Duration of programme, etc.).
Afterwards, we put them in a user-friendly app created using Python for Sport Singapore to use
